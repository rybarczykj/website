For my midterm project, I made a T-Shirt generator. The grammar generates some text, a description of an image, and a general style idea, then I designed the shirt.

The idea was to make shirts that look like real shirts, but feature generally nonsensical text-- an idea loosely inspired by the comedic mistranslations and gibberish sentences which occur with aestheticizing the look of a different language.

To achieve a wide variety of this pseudo-english gibberish, I used lyrics, specifically MF DOOM and Steely Dan, who I thought would make for an interesting vocab. Crucially, I also ended up making a corpora which combined consecutively uttered words as phrases, preserving that sense of vague understandability.
